Microsoft

Microsoft Corporation is a software company headquartered in Redmond, Washington. It is best known for:-

  1. Microsoft Windows line of operating systems
  2. Microsoft Office Suite
  3. Internet Explorer
  4. Xbox game console
  5. Microsoft Surface Series Of Tablet Computers.

It is the world's largest software maker in terms of revenue. It is the World's Third Most Valuable Company.

It was founded by Bill Gates and Paul Allen. Its current CEO is Satya Nadella.
